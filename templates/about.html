Phase 1: Detection

On March 12, 2025, a new object, Impactor-2025, was detected by a network of ground-based telescopes. Initial orbital analysis suggests a low-probability, but non-zero, chance of Earth impact.

Project Overview

Meteor Madness is a web application created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge to provide an interactive platform for exploring asteroid impact scenarios and planetary defense strategies. By integrating real data from NASA and a simplified physics engine, we aim to educate users about the science behind these events and empower them to understand the importance of planetary defense initiatives.
